FAQ's

Does GITAM offer any scholarships?

Yes, GITAM offers several scholarships across different categories to support students, including Merit-based scholarships, Need-based scholarships, Sports scholarships, and Loyalty scholarships.

What is the Campus life experience at GITAM?

GITAM provides modern infrastructure, student clubs, sports facilities, cultural activities, and innovation labs.

Is GITAM University accredited by the UGC?

Yes, GITAM is recognized by UGC and accredited with high academic standards.

How does GITAM’s faculty composition benefit students?

Faculty members include experienced professors, researchers, and industry experts.

Can I visit the campus before enrolling?

Yes, students and parents can visit campuses through scheduled campus tours.

Does the university help in securing internships?

Yes, GITAM collaborates with industries and companies for internships and placements.
